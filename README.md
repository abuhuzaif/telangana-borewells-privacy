# Privacy Policy - Telangana Borewells App

**Last updated: July 3, 2026**

## 1. Information We Collect
- Location data (only when you use the booking form)
- Name, phone number, and location entered in booking form (sent via WhatsApp only)

- Location is used only to auto-fill your address in the booking form
- Booking details are sent directly to our WhatsApp — we do not store them on any server

## 3. Third Party Services

- OpenStreetMap Nominatim — for location lookup

## 4. Data Storage
- Drilling rates are stored locally on your device only
- We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers
